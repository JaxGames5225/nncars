What is a Neural Network?

A Neural Network is one of the closest representations of a human brain on a computer. A neural network does not learn on its own. It needs a teacher, which in this case will be you. A neural network processes inputs and calculates an output. In this case, the inputs are the colors in front of the car and the outputs will be which direction the car steers. below, you will be able to design, train, and test your very own neural network.
